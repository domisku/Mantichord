Instructions

This is MANTICHORD - a free to use chord progression generator.

  1. Click PLAY/PAUSE button to control the playback of music.
  2. Click NEW PROGRESSION button to generate a new chord progression.
  3. Move sliders to control tempo (BPM) and volume of music.
  4. You have various options to spice things up! Click on arrows to select different instruments, rhythmic patterns or even include drums to really help you envision the sound of your chord progression in a band context!
  5. Roman numerals below chord names indicate chord functions in a chosen key.
  6. Have fun!
